The 8th Med-CORDEX workshop was held from May 14th to 15th May in Rome (Italy).

More information about Med-CORDEX can be found [here](https://www.medcordex.eu/).

The 6th Med-CORDEX workshop aimed to review the activities of the Phase 3 of Med-CORDEX, including the so-called baseline runs performed with coupled RCSMs but also new activities.
The session titles were:

- Baseline runs
- IPCC activities
- Ocean biogechemical modelling
- Impact including coastal risks and coastal cities
- Beyond the state-of-the-art Med-CORDEX including AI
