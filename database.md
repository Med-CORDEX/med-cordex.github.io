The Med-CORDEX centralized database allowing free data access has been hosted and managed by ENEA (big thanks to Lele Lombardi) from 2010 to 2025. Unfortunatly, it is not accessible anymore.
Currently, there is no centralized and open access to the Med-CORDEX data. Please contact the individually the data producing teams to access the data. More information will come when a better solution will become available.
Contact of the data producing team can be found on the home page https://med-cordex.github.io/
